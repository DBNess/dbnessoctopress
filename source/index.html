Thank you for visiting!

I'm currently based in New York City, where I write, speak, code, and learn.

I believe software is about humanity, so I founded CodeMontage to help coders become superheroes. I also founded and run Developers for Good.

I'd like to see more people learn to code, so I co-founded Girl Develop It to provide judgment-free environments to learn about software development.

My love of computing began with databases. I previously led data and analytics for Paperless Post and wrangled data for TheLadders, Capital IQ, and WealthEngine.

I am interested in speaking to help make data, analytics, open source software, and technology in general more accessible. I'd also love to hear about anything you really, really appreciate in life.
