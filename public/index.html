Thank you for visiting!

I currently live in New York City, where I help developers improve their skills while improving the world through CodeMontage. I also founded and run Developers for Good.

I’d like to see more people learning to code, so I co-founded Girl Develop It to provide judgment-free, low-cost environments to learn about software development. I previously led data and analytics for Paperless Post.

I am interested in speaking to help make data, analytics, open source software, and technology in general more accessible. I’d also love to hear about anything you really, really appreciate in life.

Feel free to reach out via Ohours, Twitter @DBNess, or email.
