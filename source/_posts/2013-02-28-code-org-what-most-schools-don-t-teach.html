The programmers of tomorrow are the wizards of the future. You're going to look like you have magic powers...

- Gabe Newell, Founder of Valve

Code.org just launched with the vision that every student in every school should have the opportunity to learn how to code as well as a film encouraging every student to do so.

I'm grateful to have contributed to this inspiring short film by Lesley Chilcott, producer of Waiting for Superman, alongside Mark Zuckerberg, Bill Gates, Bronwen Grimes, Chris Bosh, Will.I.Am, Elena Silenok, Jack Dorsey, Drew Houston, Ruchi Sanghvi, and other oustanding people.

If you're a teacher, know a teacher, sit next to a teacher on the subway, or otherwise have means to spread the word, please do so! No one is too old or too... anything, really, to learn how to code. There are several lengths and versions of the film available, and you can start learning to code right away.

Once you're ready, we'll be thrilled to have you improve the world by contributing to social good coding projects at CodeMontage.
